About Me

I have specialized in scientific computing and signal processing. During my academic career, I have worked on several projects in the field of computer graphics. I currently work at the German Aerospace Center, where I develop CoolingGen, a parametric CAD modelling tool for turbine blade and vane cooling geometries. The software uses common computer graphics methods such as surface-surface intersection and ray-marching on NURBS objects. I am qualified in Fourier analysis in the scope of Digital Signal Processing and its applications, to which I count filtering, spectrography, tomography, image reconstruction and compression and many more.
